Oatmeal Recipe

Description

Oatmeal is a simple recipe. You put oatflakes into a bowl, add nuts, berries, apples, cinnamon or cocoa powder, or anything else you like, pour boiling water into the bowl, and you have a delicious and filling meal in less than 5 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Oatflakes
  • Berries
  • Other fruit
  • Cinnamon or cocoa powder or some other spice
  • A sweetener is optional
  • Whatever else you feel like adding to it, it's freestyle

Steps

  1. Put all ingredients in a bowl
  2. Pour boiling water into bowl
  3. Wait a minute or two
  4. Eat
