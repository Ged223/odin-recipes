Soup Recipe

Description

Soup is a simple recipe. You put legumes, vegetables, potatoes, or anything else into a pot with water and you boil it until all ingredients are edible.

Ingredients

  • Some protein, like tofu, beans (or any other legumes)
  • Some starches, like potatoes or rice or stuff like couscous or barley groats
  • A lot of vegetables, frozen or fresh
  • Some onion and/or garlic
  • Whatever else you feel like adding to it, it's freestyle
  • Oil is voluntary

Steps

  1. Put all ingredients in pot
  2. Add water until all ingredients are submerged
  3. Turn on the heat
  4. Let it boil for a while
  5. Taste with spoon every 10 minutes
  6. Turn off heat once done
  7. Eat
