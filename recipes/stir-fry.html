Plant-Based Stir Fry Recipe

Description

Stir Fry is a simple recipe, where you take your ingredients, dump em in a pan(with or without oil), stir for a short while, then eat it.

Ingredients

  • Some protein, like tofu, beans (or any other legumes)
  • Some starches, like potatoes or rice or stuff like couscous or barley groats
  • A lot of vegetables, frozen or fresh
  • Some onion and/or garlic
  • Whatever else you feel like adding to it, it's freestyle
  • Oil is voluntary

Steps

  1. Put all ingredients in a pan or pot
  2. Turn on the heat
  3. Stir for a while
  4. Turn off heat
  5. Eat
